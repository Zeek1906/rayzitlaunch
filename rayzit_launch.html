Day 1
Day 1 – Activate Leadership
▶ Video 1:37
›
Before you ask anyone to give, you need your leaders to go first. Get organizational leaders to personally use the Flowpage, sign up for RAYZIT, and start a round-up plan so they experience the technology firsthand. Everything that follows in the 7-day plan is stronger when leadership has skin in the game.
Day 1 Actions
  • 1Have each leader in your organization create an account and sign up for the Round Up feature
  • 2Walk them through the RAYZIT platform. Host a quick meeting if needed.
  • 3Share your Flowpage link or QR code with every leader in your organization
  • 4Watch the video for your exact leadership activation script
TipLeadership buy-in is your most powerful asset. When your leaders are personally using RAYZIT the rest of your community will follow.
✓
Day 2
Day 2 – Build Your Story
▶ Video 1:29
›
People don't give to technology. They give to purpose. Today, you define yours. Help the organization clarify and articulate their 'why' and connect that story to the emotional impact RAYZIT will create for their mission.
Day 2 Actions
  • 1Think about what your organization is trying to accomplish
  • 2Identify projects that keep getting delayed because the funding wasn't there
  • 3Write out your organization's 'why' in two to three sentences and tie it directly to what RAYZIT makes possible
  • 4Watch the video for story-building guidance
TipYour story is what separates a fundraiser from a movement. Take the time today to get it right. Use it as the foundation for everything you share this week.
✓
Day 3
Day 3 – Launch to Inner Circle
▶ Video 1:35
›
Your first wave of support doesn't come from the public. It comes from the people who already believe in you. Direct leadership to share the Flowpage with their closest network: family, close friends, and core supporters, to generate early momentum and social proof.
Day 3 Actions
  • 1Identify 10 people from your inner circle – family, friends and core supporters
  • 2Send them a personal text or call (not a group message) inviting them to join your RAYZIT movement
  • 3Share with them your RAYZIT Flowpage link or QR code and ask them to connect today
  • 4Watch the video for your exact outreach script
TipPeople give because someone they care about asked them to. A personal ask from you to your inner circle is the most powerful first move you can make.
✓
Day 4
Day 4 – Community Sharing
▶ Video 1:31
›
Your inner circle is in. Now it's time to go wide. Expand the campaign beyond the inner circle using social media, email, text outreach, QR codes at events, and every available channel to drive broad awareness.
Day 4 Actions
  • 1Email to your full supporter list with the Flowpage serving as your central link
  • 2Post this message on social media platforms with the Flowpage link
  • 3Keep your message simple and human. Be sure to include your "why" from Day 2
  • 4Text messages to community members
  • 5Print Flowpage QR code on flyers, banners, event pamphlets, every channel possible
TipYour Flowpage should be circulating actively across your community. Keep track of who is engaging and who has not responded yet.
✓
Day 5
Day 5 – Momentum Expansion
▶ Video 1:30
›
People follow momentum. Show them it's already moving. Amplify existing activity by celebrating early wins publicly and increasing visibility through the same channels used on Day 4.
Day 5 Actions
  • 1Post again on social media sharing the progress made
  • 2Send a follow up email to your supporter list
  • 3Put your QR code in new places you haven't hit yet
  • 4Ask your inner circle to share the Flowpage link in their own networks
TipDay 5 is about keeping the energy alive and growing. Celebrate what's been built, invite more in and stay visible.
✓
Day 6
Day 6 – Re-Engagement Push
▶ Video 1:28
›
Not everyone responds to the first ask. Today, you go back for the ones who haven't yet. Reconnect personally with supporters who haven't signed up yet by reminding them of the organization's mission and the role RAYZIT plays in achieving it.
Day 6 Actions
  • 1Send a short personal message, not a mass blast
  • 2Reference your 'why' from Day 2
  • 3Reach out to people from Day 3 and Day 4 who haven't responded
  • 4Remind them what the organization is working toward and what consistent support through RAYZIT makes possible
TipToday is your last major push before the finish. Make the personal reach-out. Remind people of the mission. Make signing up feel easy.
✓
Day 7
Day 7 – Finish Strong
▶ Video 1:37 Final Day
›
Seven days in. Let's talk about what you've built and why it matters beyond this week. Drive urgency by celebrating impact made so far and reinforcing the power of RAYZIT's ongoing round-up feature to create continuous, compounding support.
Day 7 Actions
  • 1Post again on social media sharing the progress made
  • 2Reference the progress you've made
  • 3Let people know that joining now means their round-ups start working for your mission starting today
  • 4Share your Flowpage one more time across all channels
TipProtect what you've built. Stay engaged with your supporters. Keep sharing your Flowpage and let the platform keep working for you every single day.
